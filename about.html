About

Multiplicity is a digital curation on the internet's place in our lives, on both a personal & socio-political level, as young people in different parts of the world. We critically examine the internet's influence, and explore ways to build a healtheir and more equitable internet. For more on the project's inception & goals, read our origin story.



Team

We are students & alumni of NYU Abu Dhabi & NYU with backgrounds ranging from Literature & Visual Arts to Economics & Computer Engineering. We come from Palestine, Jordan, Egypt, Lebanon, Nepal, Bosnia & Herzgovina, New Zealand, South Korea, and the US.
